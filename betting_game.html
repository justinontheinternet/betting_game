Let's Play the Money-Making Game!

Instructions:

  • Your bank starts at 100 points.
  • Wager between 5 and 10 points.
  • Enter a number between 1 and 10.
  • If your number matches the one chosen by the computer, the wager will be added to your bank.
  • If your guess is off by one, it's a draw and your bank stays the same.
  • If you're off by more than one number, the wager is deducted from your bank.
